Our story

We go places.
We build things.

Bishmates is an adventure platform built by people who were tired of bad tools ruining good trips.

It started the same way every good idea does: something was missing, and nobody had fixed it yet.

Planning a trip meant bouncing between a notes app, a spreadsheet, a maps tab, a gear spreadsheet, and a group chat that had the important thing buried three hundred messages deep. Nothing talked to each other. Nothing remembered what you packed last time. Nothing cared about the actual experience.

So we started building.

"Make tools for people who actually go — not people who plan to someday."

What we're building

Bishmates is the home for a suite of adventure apps. Not a social network. Not a gear store. Just useful, honest tools that make the hard parts of going somewhere easier — so you can spend more time on the good parts.

Gearmates

Your packing co-pilot. Build trip lists, track your gear, and share kits with anyone going with you. Available now on iOS.

How we build

We're privacy-first. No analytics SDKs. No advertising. No selling your data. When you use our apps without an account, nothing leaves your device. When you create an account, your data lives on secure infrastructure and is used for exactly one thing: making your experience work.

We're also small, which means we're careful. Every feature we ship is something we needed ourselves. We'd rather do a few things very well than everything poorly.
