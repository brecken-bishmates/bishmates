Gearmates — frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Gearmates?

No. Gearmates works fully without an account — all your gear and trip data is saved locally on your device. Create an account only if you want to sync across multiple devices.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at support@bishmates.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days. You can also request a copy of your data before deletion.

My data isn't syncing between devices. What should I do?

Make sure you're signed in to the same account on both devices. Try signing out and back in to force a sync. If the issue persists, contact us with your account email and device details.

How does the "Find nearby shop" feature work?

When you tap "Find nearby shop," the app requests your device's GPS location one time to show gear stores in your area. Your location is held in memory only for that session — it's never stored or transmitted to our servers. You can deny location permission and the rest of the app continues to work normally.

I signed in with Google but can't access my account. What do I do?

Make sure you're using the same Google account you originally used to sign up. If you're still having trouble, email us at support@bishmates.com with the email address associated with your Google account.

Can I export my gear list or trip data?

We're working on a data export feature. In the meantime, email us at support@bishmates.com and we can manually export your data in a readable format.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports — they genuinely help. Email support@bishmates.com with a description of what happened, what you expected to happen, and your iOS version and device model.

When is Waymates launching?

Waymates is actively in development. Join the waitlist on the Apps page to be first to know when it's ready.
