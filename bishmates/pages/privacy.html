1. Overview

Bishmates ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Bishmates website at bishmates.com and the Gearmates iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

The short version: Gearmates works fully without an account — in that case, nothing leaves your device. If you choose to create an account, your gear and trip data is stored securely on our servers so you can sync across devices. We do not run advertising, we do not sell your data, and we have no analytics or tracking SDKs of any kind.

2. What data we collect

Without an account

When you use Gearmates without creating an account, all data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No data is transmitted to our servers or to any third party. This includes your gear inventory, trip lists, and preferences.

With an account

When you create an account, the following data is collected and stored on our servers:

Data type What it includes Linked to you?
Account identity Email address, authentication provider (email/password or Google), account creation timestamp, last sign-in timestamp Yes
Profile Username (defaults to your email prefix), home base city (text you type), preferred units (km/mi), preferred currency, notification settings Yes
Gear inventory Item names, notes, category, condition, photos (if added), purchase links, acquisition dates, trip assignments Yes
Trip data Trip name, destination, dates, packing lists (item names, statuses, prices, assignments, purchase links) Yes

App Store privacy label summary

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, the following data types are collected and linked to your identity when you use a Gearmates account:

  • Contact info (email address)
  • User content (gear inventory, trip data)
  • Identifiers (user ID)

No data is collected for tracking purposes. No data is shared with third parties for advertising.

3. How we use your data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Gearmates service. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To sync your gear and trip data across your devices
  • To provide app features that depend on your preferences (units, currency, home base)
  • To respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-party services

Gearmates uses a small number of third-party services to function. Each has access only to the data necessary for its role:

Service Purpose Data it receives
Supabase Database and authentication (hosted on AWS infrastructure) Account credentials, gear/trip data for signed-in users
Google OAuth Optional "Sign in with Google" authentication Your Google identity token (name, email) if you choose this sign-in method
Nominatim / OpenStreetMap City name autocomplete during onboarding Your typed search query and IP address
Google Fonts Typography on the Bishmates website IP address, browser user agent, and referrer URL on each page load

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, A/B testing services, or any behavioral tracking of any kind.

5. Location data

Gearmates requests access to your device's GPS location for one specific feature: "Find nearby shop," which shows gear stores near your current position. This access is:

  • On-demand only — location is requested only when you open the "Find nearby shop" sheet
  • Transient — coordinates are held in memory for that session only and discarded when you close the sheet
  • Not transmitted — your GPS coordinates are never sent to our servers
  • Not stored — coordinates are never written to local storage or your account

Separately, you may type a home base city during onboarding. This is a text string (not GPS data) that is stored in your profile to personalize the experience. You can change or delete it at any time in your account settings.

6. Data storage & security

Account data is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure. Supabase employs industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). All data is stored in the United States.

Without an account, all data is stored only on your device using the browser's localStorage API and never transmitted anywhere.

7. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us at privacy@bishmates.com. Upon deletion, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Without an account, data persists in your device's localStorage until you clear it through your browser or device settings.

8. Children's privacy

Gearmates is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bishmates.com. We will respond within 30 days.
